# TREND RADAR — First Report
**Date:** April 5, 2026  
**Scope:** AI Consulting, Automation, Quebec Market

## 🎯 TRENDS TO WATCH (HIGH SIGNAL)

### 1. **AI Consulting Market Maturation**
- **Signal:** Shift from "AI hype" to "AI implementation"
- **Evidence:** Companies now want ROI, not just demos
- **Opportunity:** Fractional CAIO model validated (John Cheney → $2.5M in 12 months)
- **Risk:** Competition increasing as market matures

### 2. **Vibe Coding Acceleration**
- **Signal:** Non-coders building apps in days, not months
- **Evidence:** Replit, Bolt.new, Lovable enabling rapid prototyping
- **Opportunity:** Build internal tools for clients as part of service
- **Risk:** Barrier to entry lowering → more competition

### 3. **Quebec AI Adoption Lag**
- **Signal:** French-speaking market 12-18 months behind US
- **Evidence:** Limited AI expertise in traditional industries
- **Opportunity:** First-mover advantage in Quebec manufacturing/construction
- **Risk:** Education curve longer

## ⚠️ RISKS & THREATS

### **Market Risks:**
- AI hype cycle cooling → budget cuts
- Open-source AI reducing need for consultants
- Economic downturn affecting tech spending

### **Competitive Risks:**
- US firms entering Quebec market
- Tech giants (Google, Microsoft) offering AI consulting
- Freelancers undercutting prices

### **Mitigation Strategy:**
- Build deep industry expertise (construction/manufacturing)
- Focus on ROI, not technology
- Develop proprietary tools/playbooks
