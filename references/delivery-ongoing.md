# Delivery — Ongoing (Month 2+)

## Monthly Rhythm

### Week 1: Strategy & Planning
- Review month-end metrics
- Plan priorities for this month
- Schedule training sessions

### Week 2: Implementation
- Build new automations
- Optimize existing systems
- Address escalated issues

### Week 3: Training & Adoption
- Group training sessions
- 1:1 coaching for power users
- New tool rollouts

### Week 4: Review & Reporting
- Monthly strategy presentation
- ROI metrics review
- Next month planning

## Ongoing Deliverables

### For $10K/mo Client (Growth)

**Monthly includes:**
- Strategy call (1 hr)
- Automation improvements (4-6 hrs)
- Team training (2-3 hrs)
- Dashboard maintenance
- Ad-hoc support (3-4 hrs)

**Typical month:**
- 1-2 new automations
- 1 training session
- Performance review
- Strategy recommendations

### For $15K/mo Client (Scale)

**Monthly includes:**
- Strategy call (1-2 hrs)
- More complex automations (8-10 hrs)
- Team training (4-6 hrs)
- Dashboard builds
- Ad-hoc support (5-8 hrs)

**Typical month:**
- 3-4 new automations
- 2 training sessions
- New dashboard features
- Competitive analysis

### For $25K/mo Client (Enterprise)

**Monthly includes:**
- Weekly check-ins
- Full team management
- Multiple department implementations
- Custom builds
- Priority support

## Common Ongoing Projects

4. **Inventory management** — Predictive ordering
5. **HR onboarding** — Automated paperwork, training

7. **Meeting notes** — Transcription + action items

1. **Dashboard enhancements** — New metrics, better UI
2. **Data quality** — Clean up CRM, better inputs
3. **Integration expansion** — Connect more systems
4. **Security** — Better access controls, backups
5. **Training refresh** — New features, advanced topics

## Client Health Metrics

### Track Monthly
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Dashboard logins | >80% of users, 3x/week |
| Automations used | All live ones active |
| Training attendance | >80% |
| Support tickets | <5 critical/month |
| NPS score | >7 |
| Renewal rate | >95% |

### Red Flags
- Dashboard not used for 2+ weeks
- No response to emails/calls
- Support tickets increasing
- Training attendance dropping
- Complaints about value

---

## Scaling Delivery

### When to Add Resources
- Client needs exceed 20 hrs/month
- Technical complexity increases
- Multiple departments involved
- Client requests faster turnaround

### Options
1. **Add hours within existing contract** — Simplest
2. **Upgrade tier** — Formal scope expansion
3. **Add contractor** — For specific expertise

---

## Renewal Process

### 60 Days Before Renewal
- Schedule planning call
- Review year's results
- Discuss goals for next year

### 30 Days Before Renewal
- Send renewal proposal
- Include new value adds
- Address any concerns

### At Renewal
- Celebrate wins
- Present ROI metrics
- Confirm continued scope
- Update contract

### If Client Wants to Cancel
- Understand why
- Offer transition plan
- Ask for feedback
- Request referral
- Document lessons learned
