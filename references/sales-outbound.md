# Sales — Cold Outreach Playbook

## Finding Prospects

### Research Tool Protocol
Use Perplexity or Manus to find 20 businesses fitting your target:
- Revenue: $10M-$50M
- No CTO
- 50-300 employees
- Industry: Construction, manufacturing, professional services, healthcare, real estate

**Search query example:**
"Find 20 local businesses in Montreal area with $10M-$50M revenue, 50-300 employees, no CTO"

### Sources
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator (free trial)
- Google Maps (search for industry + city)
- Inc. 5000 Canada list
- Industry associations
- Local business directories

## LinkedIn Outreach

### Step 1: Connection Request
**Profile:** Find CEO/owner, read their profile for personalization
**Message (under 300 chars):**
```
Hey [First Name], I help [industry] companies like yours implement AI to cut costs and stay ahead. Quick question: are you currently using AI in your business?
```

### Step 2: Follow-up (after accepted)
```
Thanks for connecting! I've noticed [specific observation about their business - e.g., "your team seems to be growing"]. We're helping companies in [their industry] automate [specific function] and save 20-30% on operational costs. Would a 15-minute call be worth your time to see if there's a fit?
```

### Step 3: If No Response After 5 Days
```
Hey [Name], just checking if you had a chance to see my last message. Happy to share some examples of what we've done for similar companies if helpful.
```

## Text/Call to Warm Contacts

### For People You Know (Loosely)
**Text:**
```
Hey [Name]! It's Michel. I started a new venture - AI consulting for businesses. Basically helping companies like yours figure out AI so they don't get left behind. Any chance you know any business owners who might be interested? Or want to grab coffee and I'll show you what I'm seeing?
```

**Call (if they don't text back):**
"Hi [Name], it's Michel. Quick question - you probably saw my text about the AI consulting thing. I know things are busy, just wanted to see if you knew anyone who might benefit from this. No pressure either way."

## Email Outreach

**Subject:** Quick question about AI
**Body:**
```
Hi [Name],

I help [industry] companies implement AI to cut costs and stay competitive.

Most businesses we talk to are in the same boat: they know AI matters but don't have anyone internally to lead the charge. That's where we come in.

Would a 15-minute call be worth your time to see if there's a fit?

Best,
Michel
```

## What to Say When They Respond

### They Say "Not Interested"
"That's totally fair. When you think about AI for your business, what's the biggest thing holding you back?" (Listen, then respond)

### They Say "We're Already Using AI"
"That's great! Most companies are using ChatGPT as a fancy Google search. What we're doing is building custom systems - dashboards, automations, AI agents - that actually transform how you operate. Would love to show you what's possible."

### They Say "Send me info"
"Absolutely. Can I ask - what specifically are you most interested in? That way I can send you something relevant rather than a generic deck."

### They Want to Know Pricing
"It depends on scope, but typically our clients invest $10K-$15K/month for ongoing fractional CAIO services. That includes strategy, implementation, and training. Want me to walk you through what's included?"
