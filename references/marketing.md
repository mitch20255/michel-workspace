Most companies know AI matters but don't have anyone internally to lead the charge. That's where I come in.

As your Fractional Chief AI Officer, I:
→ Build AI strategies aligned to your business

Let's talk about how AI can transform your business.

- Previous relevant experience

## Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: Show the Work (60%)
- What you're building for clients
- Screenshots (with permission)
- Results and metrics
- "Here's what we did this week"

### Pillar 2: Education (25%)
- AI tips and tricks
- Tool comparisons
- Industry trends
- "Here's what you should know"

### Pillar 3: Behind the Scenes (15%)
- Your journey
- Lessons learned
- Personal (hobbies, life)
- "Here's what I'm thinking"

## Posting Cadence

### Minimum Viable
- 3 posts per week
- 1 newsletter per month

### Optimal
- 1 post per day
- 2-3 newsletters per week
- Comments on others' posts

### Never Miss
- Monday: Motivation/Week planning
- Wednesday: Education/Tips
- Friday: Results/Week wrap-up

## Post Types That Work

### 1. Client Results (Best performer)
```
Just helped [Company] cut their reporting time by 80%.

Before: 5 hours/week manually compiling reports
After: AI generates them automatically in 10 minutes

That's 4.5 hours/week saved. 234 hours/year.

The best part? Their team actually enjoys using it now.

What process in YOUR business is eating up too much time?
```

### 2. Quick Tips
```
3 ChatGPT prompts every business owner should know:

1. "Act as a CEO. Analyze this data and give me 3 strategic recommendations: [paste data]"

2. "Write a cold outreach email to [target] about [product] that's [benefit], using [tone]"

3. "Review this contract and identify 3 risks I should be concerned about: [paste contract]"

Save this. You'll need it.
```

### 3. Behind the Scenes
```
I spent 3 days building what used to take 6 months and $100K.

Last year, I watched a founder get a $100K quote for a simple app.
I told him to try Replit instead.
20 minutes later, he had a working prototype.

3 days, $400 total cost, and it now serves 10,000 users.

The cost to start a tech business has dropped from millions to the cost of a nice dinner.

If you're a business owner not using AI to build, you're falling behind.
```

### 4. Questions/Engagement
```
What's the #1 thing holding you back from implementing AI in your business?

For me, 2 years ago, it was "I don't know how to code."
Then I learned: you don't need to.

Drop your biggest obstacle below 👇
```

### 5. Framework/Methodology
```
The STE Framework I use on every client call:

S - Strategy: "Are you using AI? You're right where everyone else is. We help you get ahead."

T - Transformation: "Anyone you need to hire? What if AI did it for $25/month instead of $8K/month?"

E - Education: "What if your whole team was 20% more productive?"

By the end, they're thinking:
1. I need to stay ahead (Strategy)
2. I can save money (Transformation)
3. My team will benefit (Education)

Then: "We'll do all this for $15K/month."

Works every time.
```

## Newsletter Strategy

### Weekly Newsletter Format

**Subject:** [Number] thing I learned this week / [Topic] update

**Structure:**
1. Quick insight from the week (2-3 sentences)
2. Main content (1-2 paragraphs)
3. One tool or tip
4. One question for the reader

### Newsletter Topics
- AI tool reviews
- Client case studies (anonymized)
- Industry trends
- Personal learnings
- Book/podcast recommendations

## Referral System

### Ask for Referrals
After every successful delivery:
"By the way, do you know any other business owners who might benefit from what we do?"

### Make it Easy
- Send introduction email for them
- Give them referral bonus
- Make it low-touch

### Track Referrals
- Source: Who referred
- Status: Reached out/Met/Closed
- Bonus: What incentive offered

## Facebook (The Hidden Gem)

**Why it works:**
- Personal network of 300-1000 friends
- 10% are business owners = 30-100 potential clients
- No algorithm fighting
- Just tell people what you do

**What to post:**
- Same content as LinkedIn
- Less polished, more personal
- 1x per week on personal page
- "I just started helping businesses implement AI..."
