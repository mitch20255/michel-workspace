# Delivery — First Month (Month 1)

## Month 1 Priorities

### Week 1-2: Quick Win — CEO Dashboard
**Goal:** Make the CEO say "This is already worth it."

**What to build:**
1. Connect to their existing systems:
   - CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, etc.)
   - Accounting (QuickBooks, Xero, etc.)
   - Project management (Asana, Monday, etc.)
   - Communication (Slack, Teams, email)

2. Build a single dashboard showing:
   - Revenue metrics
   - Pipeline/lead status
   - Key operational metrics
   - Custom metrics they care about

3. Add AI layer:
   - Natural language queries ("How did we do last quarter?")
   - Custom report generation
   - Anomaly detection

**Tools:** Replit, Retool, or simple Next.js + API connections

**Time:** 2-3 days

---

### Week 2-3: Process Automation
**Goal:** Show visible time/money savings

**Identify top 3-5 manual processes:**
Common automations:
1. **CRM Data Entry**
   - Slack → CRM integration
   - Team types in Slack, AI logs to CRM
   
2. **Lead Qualification**
   - AI agent researches inbound leads
   - Enriches CRM records automatically
   
3. **Weekly Reporting**
   - AI generates reports from data
   - Emails to leadership automatically
   
4. **Scheduling & Follow-ups**
   - AI manages appointment booking
   - Automated reminder sequences

**How to identify what to automate:**
Ask: "What's the most repetitive task your team does every day?"
Ask: "What takes the most time but doesn't generate revenue?"
Ask: "What do you wish you had more time for?"

**Deliver one automation by end of Week 3.**

---

### Week 3-4: Team Training
**Goal:** Make every employee 20% more productive

**Training Session Structure:**

**Part 1: Break the Ice (30 min)**
- Have everyone build a game in Replit (Tetris, Flappy Bird)
- Why? Removes fear of AI, builds confidence
- "If you can build a game, you can build anything"

**Part 2: Practical Skills (1 hour)**
- ChatGPT for their specific role:
  - Sales: Write emails, follow-ups, proposals
  - Operations: Analyze data, write processes
  - Customer Service: Respond faster, consistency
  - Marketing: Content, social, ads
  
- Prompt library for each role
- Best practices: always edit AI output

**Part 3: Vibe Coding Intro (30 min)**
- Show them how to build simple tools
- "I can't write code but I can describe what I want"
- Give them permission to try

---

### Week 4: Monthly Strategy Presentation
**Goal:** Position yourself as strategic advisor

**Presentation Structure:**

1. **Industry Update** (5 min)
   - What's new in AI that affects their industry
   - Competitors doing with AI
   - Trends to watch

2. **Results This Month** (5 min)
   - Dashboard usage stats
   - Time saved from automation
   - Team adoption metrics

3. **Next Month's Plan** (5 min)
   - New automation opportunities
   - Training focus
   - Strategic initiatives

## What NOT to Do in Month 1

- ❌ Don't try to automate everything at once
- ❌ Don't build complex systems before proving value
- ❌ Don't overwhelm the team with too much training
- ❌ Don't disappear between calls
- ❌ Don't over-engineer the dashboard
- ❌ Don't take on technical debt with quick hacks

## If Things Go Wrong

### Client is unhappy with pace
"Weeks 1-2 are about foundation. By Week 3-4 you'll see the full value. Let's adjust our weekly check-ins to make sure we're aligned."

### Client wants more than scope
"I'd love to help with that. That's an additional [amount] and would require expanding our scope. Want me to add it to next month?"

### Technical issues
"Let me troubleshoot this. I'll get back to you within [X] hours with a solution."

### Team not using tools
"Remember, the goal is to make their lives easier, not add work. Let me adjust the training to focus on their specific needs."
