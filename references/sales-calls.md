# Sales — Discovery Calls

## Pre-Call Preparation

### Research Before Every Call
- Company website
- LinkedIn profile of caller
- Recent news/press
- Their industry trends
- Potential pain points

### Set Up
- Quiet space
- Notebook open
- Calendar with next slots ready
- Water nearby

---

## Call Structure (30 minutes)

### Minute 0-2: Rapport & Discovery
**Opener:**
"Hey [Name], thanks for making time to chat. Before we dive in, can you tell me a bit about yourself and what brought you to this conversation?"

**Listen for:**
- Their role
- Company size
- Current challenges
- What they hope to get

### Minute 2-5: Assess Current State
**The Key Question:**
"Are you guys using AI right now? And if so, how?"

**Their answer will likely be:**
- "We use ChatGPT sometimes"
- "We've thought about it"
- "We have no idea where to start"

**Response to each:**
- "You’re right where everybody else is. The fact that you’re even on this call means you’re already ahead."

### Minute 5-10: Strategy (STE Part 1)
**Pillar: Strategy — Speak to the fear of falling behind**

Say something like:
"What we do is come in and help you understand how AI is going to affect your industry, what your competitors are doing, and what you can do to stay ahead. Most CEOs we talk to feel like they're playing catch-up. We help you get ahead of the curve."

**Ask:**
"What keeps you up at night when it comes to technology and AI?"

### Minute 10-15: Transformation (STE Part 2)
**Pillar: Transformation — Show the ROI**

**The Money Question:**
"Is there anybody you've been wanting to hire? Some task or role you need filled?"

**Listen for common answers:**
- Marketing coordinator
- Operations manager
- Data analyst
- Customer service rep
- Admin staff

**Response:**
"That person was going to cost you $8,000/month. What if we set up something that costs $25/month to run, works 24/7, and does it automatically?"

**Then layer on more:**
- Dashboard for the CEO
- Automated CRM data entry
- AI-generated reports
- Lead qualification bots

**Each adds more ROI justification.**

### Minute 15-20: Education (STE Part 3)
**Pillar: Education — Expand the value**

"Now look, we're already making a huge impact. But what if we could get 20% more productivity out of the rest of your existing staff? Your [X] employees that are already on payroll? How much more revenue would you make if everyone was 20% more efficient?"

**Math example:**
On a $20M business, that's $4M in implied value. Your $15K/month is a tiny fraction.

### Minute 20-25: Package & Close
**Present the options:**

"Here's what I recommend based on what you've told me:

**AI Jumpstart** (one-time): $5K-$15K for initial assessment, training day, and roadmap. Good for companies not ready for ongoing.

**Growth** ($10K/month): Strategy + light automation + monthly training. For $10-$20M businesses.

**Scale** ($15K/month): Full STE framework + dashboard builds + team coaching. For $20-$40M businesses.

**Enterprise** ($25K/month): Dedicated fractional CAIO, multiple department implementation.

Which resonates most with you?"

### Minute 25-30: Handle Objections & Close

**If they want to think about it:**
"Absolutely. What specifically do you want to think through? Maybe I can help clarify."

**If they want to talk to partners:**
"Who else is involved? Would it make sense to get them on a call so we can all be aligned?"

**If they want to start small:**
"Great. Let's do the Jumpstart to get started, then we can expand to monthly. That way you see the value firsthand."

## Technical Credibility Drop

During transformation pitch, show expertise without overloading:

"Yeah, we can go into Replit, build this system, API it into your dashboard, connect it to your CRM..."

Then catch yourself:
"But you know what, you don't need to worry about that. That's why you hire us."

**Why it works:**
1. CEO thinks "this guy already has a solution in his head"
2. Reinforces they need you because technical stuff is over their head

## Common Responses & Comebacks

### "We already have an IT guy"
"That's great. This is different from traditional IT - we're focused specifically on AI implementation, which requires different expertise than general IT. Your IT person can focus on infrastructure while we handle AI."

### "We tried AI before and it didn't work"
"I hear that a lot. What was the specific challenge? Often it's about implementation rather than the technology itself. Let me ask - what was the outcome you were hoping for?"

### "My team won't use it"
"Valid concern. That's actually why our training component is so important. We don't just build systems - we get your team excited about using them. We start with fun stuff like building games to break the ice."

### "We don't have time"
"Completely understand. That's exactly why companies hire us - we're here to do the heavy lifting. You don't need to become an AI expert. You just need to see the results."

### "Can you just send me some info?"
"Absolutely. But here's what I'd recommend - let's schedule a call where I can show you specifically what we'd do for your company. Generic info doesn't tell the whole story. What if I send you a 2-minute video instead?"

## Post-Call Actions

### Immediately After Call
- [ ] Send follow-up email with key points discussed
- [ ] Add to CRM with notes
- [ ] Schedule follow-up if appropriate
- [ ] Send LinkedIn connection request if not connected

### If No Decision
- [ ] Send case study relevant to their industry
- [ ] Send article about AI in their industry
- [ ] Check in 5-7 days

### If They Said Yes
- [ ] Send contract same day
- [ ] Send invoice
- [ ] Schedule onboarding call
- [ ] Add to client delivery system

## Call Script Summary

1. **Intro:** "Thanks for making time. Tell me about yourself and what brought you here."
2. **Assess:** "Are you guys using AI right now?"
3. **Strategy:** "We're helping companies stay ahead of competitors on AI."
4. **Transformation:** "Is there anyone you need to hire? What if AI could do that for $25/mo?"
5. **Education:** "What if your whole team was 20% more productive?"
6. **Package:** Present options ($5K-$15K or $10K-$25K/month)
7. **Close:** "Here's how we sign up."
