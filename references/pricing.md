# Pricing — Packages & Strategy

## Pricing Philosophy

### From Hourly to System-Based
**Old way:** "For $10K you get 12-15 hours per month"
**New way:** "We install and manage our AI system in your business"

**Why system-based wins:**
- Creates dependency (harder to cancel)
- No switching cost calculation for client
- You're selling transformation, not time
- Scales better as you hire

## Discounts & Packaging

### Annual Discount
- **10% off** for annual contract paid upfront
- Example: $10K/mo → $9K/mo × 12 = $108K/year vs $120K

### Multi-Year
- **15% off** for 2-year contract
- **20% off** for 3-year contract

### Volume Discount
- 2+ clients: 5% off each
- 5+ clients: 10% off each

### Referral Discount
- Client refers new client: Both get 1 month free
- Partner refers: 10% ongoing commission

---

## When to Offer Discounts

### Early Stage (First 5 Clients)
- Consider 10-20% discount to land first clients
- Better to have a paying reference than perfect pricing
- But: Don't go below $7K/month for retainer

### Strong Negotiation
- If they're comparing to competitors
- If they have budget constraints
- If they're large and can become anchor client

### Never Discount
- On one-time projects (they're already discounted)
- To match competitors (you compete on value, not price)
- On your bottom tier (already accessible)

## Negotiation Scripts

### They Say "Can You Do $8K?"
"We're really aligned on value here. Let me ask - what's driving the budget concern? [Listen] Here's what I can do: We can start with Growth at $10K for the first 3 months to prove value, then adjust. Or we can narrow scope to $8K worth of work. What works better for you?"

### They Say "We Have $5K Budget"
"For that budget, I'd recommend our $5K AI Jumpstart. It's a one-time engagement that gives you the assessment, training, and roadmap. Then if you want to continue, we can discuss monthly. That way you see the value before committing."

### They Want Everything for Less
"I hear you on the budget. Here's what I'd recommend: Let's prioritize the top 2-3 things that will have the biggest impact. We can always add more as we go. Which of these is most important to you? [List 3-4 items]"

## Value Justification Math

### For $15K/month Client

**Salary Comparison:**
- 1 operations manager: $8K/mo salary + benefits = ~$11K/mo
- Your AI does more, costs less

**Revenue Impact:**
- Help close 1 extra deal/month (avg deal $50K) = $50K revenue
- Your cost: $15K

**Productivity:**
- 60 employees × 20% more productive
- If avg salary $50K = $1K employee/month value
- Total value: $60K/month

## Price Changes

### When to Raise Prices
- After 6 months with client
- When you add new capabilities
- Annually (5-10%)
- When demand exceeds capacity

### How to Communicate
- "We've updated our pricing to reflect expanded capabilities..."
- Give existing clients 60-day notice
- Grandfather old rates for 6 months
