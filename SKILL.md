## Core Business Model

### What You Sell
**Product:** Fractional Chief AI Officer (CAIO)
- Strategy: AI roadmap, competitive analysis
- Transformation: Build AI systems, automations, dashboards
- Education: Train team, vibe coding workshops

**Target Client:**
- Revenue: $10M-$50M/year
- Employees: 50-300
- No CTO / No tech leadership
- Industries: Construction, manufacturing, professional services, healthcare practices, real estate

**Pricing:**
- AI Jumpstart: $5K-$15K (one-time)
- Growth: $10K/month (12-15 hrs)
- Scale: $15K/month (20-30 hrs)
- Enterprise: $25K/month (50-60 hrs)

### Startup Costs
- ChatGPT Plus: $20/mo
- Replit Core: $25/mo
- Domain + Email: ~$20/mo
- LinkedIn Premium: $30/mo
- **Total: ~$100/mo**

## The STE Sales Framework

Use on EVERY discovery call:

1. **Strategy** (Fear: "Am I falling behind?")
   - "Are you guys using AI right now?"
   - "You’re right where everybody else is. What we do is come in and help you understand how AI will affect your industry, what your competitors are doing, and what you can do to stay ahead."

2. **Transformation** (ROI: "Can I save money?")
   - "Is there anybody you’ve been wanting to hire?"
   - "That person was going to cost $8,000/month. What if we set up something that costs $25/month to run?"

3. **Education** (Upsell: "Can my whole team benefit?")
   - "What if we could get 20% more productivity out of your existing staff? How much more revenue would you make?"

**Close:** "We’ll do all of this for $15,000/month."

## First 30 Days Checklist

### Days 1-7: Setup
- [ ] ChatGPT Plus + Replit Core accounts
- [ ] Domain + business email (Google Workspace)
- [ ] LinkedIn profile updated: "Helping [industry] companies implement AI | Fractional CAIO"
- [ ] Simple website (vibe-code in Replit)
- [ ] List of 20 target businesses (use Perplexity/Manus)
- [ ] Post first LinkedIn content

### Days 8-14: Outreach
- [ ] 5-10 messages/day to target businesses
- [ ] 3-5 LinkedIn posts
- [ ] Personal Facebook post
- [ ] Book 2-3 discovery calls
- [ ] Use STE framework on calls

### Days 15-21: Close & Deliver
- [ ] Close first client ($5K-$15K)
- [ ] Deliver CEO dashboard (quick win)
- [ ] Document results for case study
- [ ] Ask for referral

### Days 22-30: Momentum
- [ ] Continue outreach
- [ ] Post 3-5 more times
- [ ] Book 3-5 more calls
- [ ] Set 90-day goal: 3-5 retainers

## First Client Delivery (Month 1 Priority)

1. **CEO Dashboard** (Week 1-2)
   - Connect to their APIs (CRM, accounting, etc.)
   - Single pane of glass for the business
   - Interactive with AI queries

2. **Process Automation** (Week 2-3)
   - Identify top 3-5 manual processes
   - Automate: CRM data entry, lead qualification, reporting

3. **Team Training** (Week 3-4)
   - Group session: start with something fun (build a game in Replit)
   - Then practical: ChatGPT for their role
   - Goal: make everyone 20% more productive

4. **Monthly Strategy Presentation**
   - What's new in AI for their industry
   - What competitors are doing
   - Next quarter's roadmap

## What to Do When...

### Client says "too expensive"
"This is less than the cost of one full-time employee. We're giving you AI capabilities that would take a team years to develop. What's your budget?"

### Client says "not ready"
"What would need to change for you to be ready? Let's set a date 30/60/90 days out and reassess."

### Client churns
"Understand. What could we have done differently? Can we do a 90-day transition where I train someone internally?"

### You don't know the answer
"Great question. Let me research that and get back to you by [specific time]."

### Client asks for something outside scope
"I'd love to help with that. That would be an additional [amount]. Want me to add it to next month's scope?"

### No-show on call
"Hey [Name], I noticed we missed our call. Let's reschedule - I'm available [times]. Looking forward to it."
